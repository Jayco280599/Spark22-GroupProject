Project Idea

Our project idea is a long distance, self-training, automated ball thrower for dogs. An automated device in which a dog, or person, puts a rubber ball into a feeder. The system would then output a biscuit, or some other form of treat, so the dog is rewarded for returning the ball and is trained to do so. It would then feed the ball into a chamber that would launch the ball with changeable speed and trajectory for different environments.
